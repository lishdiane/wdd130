Join us for the adventure of a lifetime!

We offer half or single day white water rafting trips, or if you're looking for more adventure you can go on one of our multi-day trips.

What ages can participate?

For the half and full day trips, anyone over the age of 6 can join. For our multi-day trips, anyone over the age of 12 can join.

Do I need to have rafting experience?

No, a trained professional will be on all trips to ensure people of all skill levels can participate.

What do I need to bring?

We provide all rafting equipment (rafts, oars, pfds, etc.), camp equipment if staying overnight, and meals that will be eaten during the trip. All you need to bring are your personal items such as sunscreen, camera, sunglasses, lip balm, bathing suit, water shoes, etc.
