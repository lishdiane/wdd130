Overview

Purpose

Join us for an adventure of a lifetime. We offer single day white water rafting trips, or if you're looking for more adventure you can go on one of our multi-day trips.

Audience

Fun for the whole family. What ages can participate? Anyone over the age of 6 can enjoy our rafting trips. Do I need to have rafting experience? No, a trained professional will be on all trips to ensure people of all skill levels can participate. What do I need to bring? We provide all rafting equipment (rafts, oars, pfds, etc.) and meals that will be eaten during the trip. All you need to bring are your personal items such as sunscreen, camera, sunglasses, lip balm, bathing suit, water shoes, etc.
